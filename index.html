Why roast post?

Because supporting local business is a good thing. It’s great for the economy, keeps money and jobs local while building character and a sense of community. Small businesses also tend to recycle much of their revenue back into the local economy. Plus, Starbucks' playlist isn’t nearly as awesome.

The goal of this site is to bring awareness to some great local businesses and inform about advantages and drawbacks of each shop so you don't have to waste time on Google and Yelp. Are you already a coffee connoisseur and looking to find who sells your favorite coffee brand? Curious about who has wi-fi so you can hunker down and crank out that report that’s due on Thursday? Here’s the scoop on where to go and what to expect when you get there.

Coffee 101

Ever wonder what’s in those fancy-schmancy coffee drinks that everyone’s always ordering?
Expand your coffee horizons. Here’s what they mean:

Americano

Espresso shot (30 ml) + twice the amount of water (60 ml)

Cafe Au Lait

Brewed coffee + warm, steamed milk (instead of cold)

Cappucino

Espresso + steamed milk + foamed milk (60 ml each)

Doppio

Double espresso shot (60 ml)

Espresso

Single espresso shot (30ml)

Flat white

Espresso + milk (similar proportion of coffee to milk (Similar to latte, but contains non-frothy textured milk)

Latte

Shot of espresso + steamed milk (60, 300 ml respectively)

Macchiato

Shot of espresso (30 ml) + dot of foamed milk

Mocha

Espresso + chocolate + steamed milk (60, 60, 30 ml respectively)
